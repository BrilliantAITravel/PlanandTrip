About Brilliant AI

Introduction of the Company

Brilliant AI Travel & Expedition Pvt Ltd (Brilliant AI, hereafter), is positioned to redefine the global travel & tourism industry by bringing hyper-personalization and convenience to modern-day, tech-savvy travellers. It is a Private limited company, registered in Mainpuri, Uttar Pradesh & operated digitally with team members across India. Founded in December, 2024, Brilliant AI is currently building a team to create market presence and mature its products and services. Brilliant AI offers products and services for end-to-end travel management by building an ecosystem of intelligent, AI-driven digital products that cater to the evolving demands of modern travellers, offering personalized solutions across planning, experiencing, sharing, and even specialized events like weddings, all while prioritizing user satisfaction and leveraging advanced technology.

Mission

To empower modern travellers and lifestyle planners with hyper-personalized, seamless, and secure digital experiences, transforming the way they plan, experience, and share their travel journeys and life events. Our purpose is to make complex travel and event planning effortless, enjoyable, and uniquely tailored to each individual, leveraging cutting-edge AI.

Vision

To lead the future of hyper-personalized travel and lifestyle planning, creating a future where every journey and life event is effortlessly unique, deeply enriching, and perfectly tailored through intelligent technology. We aim to serve hundreds of millions of travellers, significantly grow our market share, and lead the development of sustainable tourism, leaving a lasting legacy in the travel world.
